Luffy Gear 4

Gear 4 Transformation

This form is activated by Luffy covering his arm in Armament Haki, biting and blowing air into it and inflating his muscular structure to increase his muscle mass (specifically his upper body) as well as inflating his fist bones, topping it all off with speeding up his blood flow and armamenting his arms and legs. This specific version of Gear 4 is known as Boundman/Bounceman, likely due to his constant bouncing, a result of being so full of air he lacks standard stability. Bounceman is capable of great strength, durability, speed and a form of flight via jumping so powerful it propels him through the air. Additionally, as with every Gear, all of Luffy's attacks get massive upgrades, with the pressure of the air in his body adding to the tension of his elasticity.

Gear 4's final form is “Snakeman”, which debuted in Episode 870 of the One Piece anime and Chapter 894 of the manga. Snakeman is somewhat similar to Bounceman, with key differences. The Armament Haki on his limbs, increased muscle mass and increased blood flow are all there, but this form favors speed and control instead of being a hulking mass of muscle. With just a slight increase of muscle mass and arm size, Snakeman is much sleeker, with the focus being on what his shielded arms can do.
